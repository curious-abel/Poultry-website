Our Story

GreenField Poultry Farm was established with a simple mission—to provide fresh, healthy, and affordable poultry products to our community. What started as a small backyard farm has grown into a trusted supplier of eggs, broilers, and poultry feeds.

Our Mission

At Agrocode Global Enterprise Nig. Ltd, our mission is to empower farmers, agribusinesses, and communities by providing world‑class poultry farming solutions. We are committed to excellence in hatchery operations, farm setup, and the supply of modern poultry equipment, ensuring sustainable food production and agricultural growth in Nigeria and beyond.

Our Vision

  • Deliver healthy high-quality day-old chicks through modern hatchery systems
  • Establish and manage poultry farms, feed mills and agro-allied infrastructure
  • Supply modern poultry equipment that improves productivity
  • Promote innovation in agriculture for long-term food security

Why Choose Us?

  • Professional hatchery and poultry expertise
  • Farm setup and agribusiness consultancy
  • Modern poultry equipment supply
  • Sustainable and innovation-driven solutions
